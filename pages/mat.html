What is MAT?

Your Business
Command Center

Most CRMs are built for enterprise sales teams. Most coaching tools are glorified spreadsheets. MAT is neither. It was built by Don Smith specifically for business owners in coaching relationships — where daily accountability and real-time visibility are everything.

Before every weekly coaching call, Don logs into your MAT dashboard and reviews exactly where you stand — your pipeline, your daily activity, your revenue. You don't spend 10 minutes catching him up. You spend 60 minutes moving forward.

That's not coaching. That's accountability. And it's what changes behavior.

Platform Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

MAT is built lean and purposeful. Every feature exists to drive one outcome — more closed revenue.

📊
Pipeline Tracker

Track every prospect and deal through every stage of your pipeline. Searchable, sortable, and always current. Know exactly where every dollar stands.

🎯
Massive Action Targets

Set your income goal and MAT calculates exactly how many calls, proposals, and closes you need every day. The Millionaire Math built into your daily routine.

🤖
Asa AI Coach

Your AI accountability coach available 24/7. Morning check-ins, objection handling practice, pipeline review, and motivation — whenever you need it.

📋
Proposals

Build and send professional proposals directly inside MAT. Tied to your pipeline so every proposal is tracked from send to close.

📅
Day View & Timeline

Plan your day from 4am to 8pm with a visual timeline. Appointments, prospecting blocks, follow-ups — your whole day in one view.

📝
EOD Reports

End-of-day accountability built into your routine. Log what you did, what you closed, and what's rolling to tomorrow. Don sees it before your next call.

Client Management

Full client and prospect database with search, filtering, and history. Know every touchpoint, every conversation, every deal with every person.

Revenue Dashboard

Real-time tracking of revenue closed vs. your income target. Weekly, monthly, and 90-day views so you always know exactly where you stand.

🎓
Coaching Portal

Weekly assignments from Don delivered inside the platform. Specific to your business, your phase, and your goals — not generic homework.
