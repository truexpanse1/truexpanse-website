Meet Ruthie

A Coach Who Knows
What It Means to Start Over

Ruthie Smith has walked her own journey of transformation — through seasons of transition, growth, and rediscovery. That lived experience is the foundation of everything she brings to her clients.

As a certified life coach, Ruthie specializes in helping women and individuals create a life that actually reflects who they are — not who they've been told to be, not who circumstances have shaped them into, but who they genuinely are at their core.

Her coaching is warm, direct, and grounded in practical action. She doesn't just help you feel good — she helps you move.
